Our Vision

With a mission to inculcate the spirit of entrepreneurship and unleash the potential of every child, Udaya SRCC is on a mission to provide the foundational knowledge of entrepreneurship and innovation in government schools.
We have designed and crafted a curriculum embedded with the nuances of entrepreneurship and skill enhancement to help these children get a taste of the world of entrepreneurship so that they can start their own entrepreneurial journey.
Right from understanding the basics of startups, valuation, social entrepreneurship to getting to know the brilliant minds behind some of the successful ventures, our initiative focuses on helping them step out of the their conventional thinking and become leaders of tomorrow.
